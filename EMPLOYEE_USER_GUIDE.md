## ğŸ” FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I mark Present multiple times?
A: No! Once you click "Mark Present", the button is disabled for that day.

### Q: What if I forget to mark attendance?
A: Don't worry! At 11:59 PM, the system will automatically mark you as "Absent".

### Q: Can I change my attendance after marking?
A: Contact your admin to make changes.

### Q: Does the system work on weekends?
A: Yes, it marks attendance every day automatically (unless configured otherwise by admin).

### Q: What does "Absent" status mean?
A: It means you didn't mark "Present" before the end of the day. Either you were absent, or forgot to mark.

### Q: How far back can I see my attendance?
A: Unlimited! The system keeps ALL your historical records forever.

### Q: What if I mark Present but I'm actually absent?
A: The system trusts your marking. You're responsible for accurate attendance.
